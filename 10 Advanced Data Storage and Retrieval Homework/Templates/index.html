Welcome to the Hawaii Climate API

Available Routes:

  • Show precipitation data: /api/v1.0/precipitation/
  • Show list of stations: /api/v1.0/stations/
  • Show year's worth of recently recorded temperatures: /api/v1.0/tobs
  • Show Min, Avg, and Max temperature for a given date range (YYYY-MM-DD): /api/v1.0/(start date) or /api/v1.0/(start)/(end)
